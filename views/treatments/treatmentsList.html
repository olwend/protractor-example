Treatments

Magical chants were often used in ancient Egyptian medicine, but the Brooklyn papyrus offers few treatments that require magic. Instead, surgery and herbal remedies are more common. Some of the herbal ingredients, such as onions, are still used in Western Africa today to treat snakebite.
